About Us

A Place Where Every Child Blossoms

Founded in 2014 in the heart of Krishna Nagar, Bharatpur, Spring Mount Day Boarding Play School was built on a simple belief — that the earliest years are the most precious. We create a warm, structured, and joyful space where Nursery, LKG, and UKG children discover the world at their own pace.

Our day boarding program means parents can trust that their child is safe, fed, engaged, and loved — all day long. Our trained staff blend structured learning with free play, art, music, and outdoor activity to nurture the whole child.
